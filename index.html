🏺 Step Pyramid of Djoser

The world’s first monumental stone building in Ancient Egypt

📜 History

The Step Pyramid of Djoser, built around 2670 BCE in Saqqara, Egypt, was designed by the architect Imhotep. It is considered the first pyramid ever built and marked a major innovation in ancient architecture.

🏗️ Structure

The pyramid was built in six stepped layers using limestone blocks. Each level was stacked smaller than the one below it, creating the iconic step design.
