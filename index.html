📜 History

The Step Pyramid of Djoser was built around 2670 BCE in Saqqara, Egypt, during the Third Dynasty. It was created for Pharaoh Djoser and designed by Imhotep, the first known architect in history.

It began as a mastaba tomb and was transformed into a six-step stone pyramid, making it the first large-scale stone monument ever built. This design influenced all later pyramids in Egypt.
