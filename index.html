🏺 Step Pyramid of Djoser

Interactive Ancient Egyptian Museum – Saqqara

📜 History

Built around 2670 BCE, the Step Pyramid of Djoser is the first major stone monument in history. It represents the transition from mudbrick tombs to monumental stone architecture.

It was designed by Imhotep for Pharaoh Djoser and symbolized eternal life and divine transformation.

🧠 Imhotep

Imhotep was an architect, priest, and physician. He is considered the world’s first named engineer and was later worshipped as a god of wisdom and medicine.

🏛️ Funerary Complex

The pyramid was part of a massive ritual complex with temples, courtyards, and symbolic spaces used for royal ceremonies and the afterlife journey.
