🏺 Step Pyramid of Djoser

Ancient Egyptian Museum Exhibit – Saqqara

📜 History

Built around 2670 BCE, this is the first large stone monument in history. Designed by Imhotep for Pharaoh Djoser.

🧠 Imhotep

First known architect in history and later worshipped as a god of wisdom and medicine.

🏛️ Funerary Complex

Included temples, courtyards, and ritual spaces for the afterlife journey.
