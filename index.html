📜 Museum Exhibit: History

The Step Pyramid of Djoser was built around 2670 BCE in Saqqara during Egypt’s Third Dynasty. It was designed by Imhotep, the world’s first recorded architect, for Pharaoh Djoser.

It began as a mastaba tomb but evolved into a six-layer stepped structure — the first large-scale stone monument ever built.
